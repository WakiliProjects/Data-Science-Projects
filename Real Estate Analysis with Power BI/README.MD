# Real Estate Marketing Analysis - Australia

![Dashboard](/Screenshot%202026-01-28%20232800.png)

## Project Overview
Business Intelligence analysis of **5,000+ properties** across major Australian cities (Brisbane, Melbourne, Perth, Sydney, Adelaide), providing actionable insights into pricing trends, market segmentation, and investment opportunities.

## Key Metrics
- **Median Property Price**: $1.04M
- **Average Property Area**: 165.69 m²
- **Properties Analyzed**: 5,000+
- **Cities Covered**: 5 major metropolitan areas

## Analysis & Insights

**Property Price by Type & Bedrooms**
- Houses command premium pricing ($1.2M+ for 2-bedroom)
- Apartments show consistent pricing (~$1.0M median) across bedroom counts
- 3-bedroom properties represent strongest market demand

**Area-Price Correlation**
- Strong positive correlation between property size and price
- Houses yield higher price per square meter than apartments
- Optimal investment range: 150-200 m² properties

**Historical Price Trends**
- Significant volatility from 1950-2020
- Notable peaks in 1960s, 1970s, and 1980s
- Recent stabilization around $1.0M median

**City Comparison**
- Minimal price variance across major cities ($1.0M - $1.05M range)
- Market equilibrium across Brisbane, Melbourne, Perth, Sydney, Adelaide
- Adelaide presents competitive entry opportunities

## Business Recommendations
✅ **Investors**: Focus on houses for appreciation potential; target 150-200 m² range  
✅ **Developers**: Prioritize 3-bedroom configurations with diversified property mix  
✅ **Marketing**: Position properties within $0.8M - $1.2M for maximum market reach
